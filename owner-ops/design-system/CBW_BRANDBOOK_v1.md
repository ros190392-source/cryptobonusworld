## Brand Accents Rule

Exchange-brand colors (Bybit orange, MEXC cyan, Binance yellow) appear **only** inside visual assets (card images, hero images, article images). They must not appear in site chrome, nav, or section styling.

---

## Typography

**Primary font:** Inter (weights: 500, 700, 800, 900)  
**Display supplement:** Barlow Condensed (700, 800) — used in visual assets only  
**Mono:** SFMono-Regular, Consolas, Liberation Mono — code chips  

**Scale:**
- H1 hero: `clamp(28px, 5vw, 44px)` — weight 900
- H2 section: `clamp(24px, 4vw, 36px)` — weight 800
- Body: 16px / 1.65 — weight 400–500
- Label/caps: 11px, weight 700, letter-spacing 0.1em
- Code chip: 13px, weight 900, monospace

## Logo

The logo uses the amber `₿` glyph combined with "Crypto" (muted white), "Bonus" (amber gradient), "World" (white).

Logo mark: `/brand/cbw-header-mark-final.png` — 54×54px, dark navy circle with amber C arc and gold sparkle stars.

---

## Voice

- Honest: Show realistic bonus ranges, not inflated maximum claims
- Precise: Date-stamp all verifications
- Direct: No "amazing" or "incredible" — just the facts
- Compliant: No "guaranteed", no fake ratings, no casino-style superlatives
