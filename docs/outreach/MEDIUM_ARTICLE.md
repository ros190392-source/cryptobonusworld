## What "Bonus" Actually Means (Most People Don't Know This)

Almost all crypto exchange bonuses are **fee vouchers**, not withdrawable cash. This is the most common source of disappointment.

Here's what typically happens:
1. You complete a task (e.g., "trade $1,000 in volume")
2. You receive a USDT voucher worth, say, $20
3. The voucher automatically deducts from your next trading fees
4. The saved fees are then released as real, withdrawable USDT

So the bonus isn't "we give you $20 you can withdraw." It's "your next $20 in trading fees is free, and the savings become cash."

For frequent traders, this is genuinely valuable. For people who plan to buy and hold without trading, the bonus is mostly irrelevant.
