## 1. Purpose

A Gold Page is the highest standard of exchange review page that CryptoBonusWorld produces.
It is not enough to have accurate content — a Gold Page must simultaneously be:

- **Search-first:** targeting the highest-value queries with the correct architecture
- **AI-answer-ready:** structured so Google AI Overviews, ChatGPT Search, and Perplexity cite it
- **Evidence-backed:** every claim verified, every screenshot approved, every bonus amount confirmed
- **Conversion-optimised:** CTA visible, promo code clear, affiliate link correct
- **Visually trustworthy:** screenshots pass human review, no errors, no wrong locale
- **Technically sound:** schema validated, canonical correct, Core Web Vitals safe
- **Compliant:** no unsafe claims, KYC rules followed, geo restrictions disclosed
- **Fresh:** dates shown, last-checked visible, stale claims removed
- **Differentiated:** unique analysis, not a templated affiliate page

**No single writer or role can reliably hit all nine dimensions at once.**

This document defines the Gold Page Specialist Squad — a pool of 31 roles (ROLE 0–30) that
collectively cover every dimension of a world-class Gold Page. The squad is not a permanent
committee. It activates on demand, per page, under the Chief Project Owner's direction.

Additionally, this document defines the **Weekly Training Protocol**: a structured cadence
by which every specialist monitors their domain, updates their knowledge, and contributes
weekly inputs to the improvement engine.

**Core Production Team (ROLES 2–13)** is active on every page production.  
**Gold Page Specialist Squad (ROLES 14–30)** activates for money pages, high-priority targets,
and any page that requires screenshot captures, offer validation, or human visual review.  
**Specialist Squad roles are cumulative** — activating ROLE 20 does not deactivate ROLE 6.

### Weekly Training Boundary

Weekly training produces **recommendations and flags**, not autonomous production changes.

Specifically:
- ROLE 14 identifies a SERP opportunity → **must become a sprint task** before implementation
- ROLE 15 identifies an AI answer gap → **must become a content recommendation** before editing
- ROLE 16 identifies an offer mismatch → **must become a flag** for ROLE 0 decision
- ROLE 17 captures a screenshot → **must go to `reports/`** and await post-production + owner review
- ROLE 25 identifies a stale claim → **must become a refresh task** before editing evidence

No specialist role may directly modify production data (`src/data/exchanges.json`,
`src/data/evidence/*.json`, `src/data/content-overrides.json`) without going through the
standard sprint task pipeline and getting the required approval gates.

The Chief Project Owner is the only role that can override a no-autopilot gate, in writing,
as part of a named sprint task.

## 11. Chain of Checks for Gold Page

Every Gold Page must pass all 16 checks in sequence before the QA/release approval is given.
No check may be skipped. If a check fails, the page returns to the responsible role and
does not proceed until the check passes.

| Step | Check | Owner Role | Pass Condition | Fail Action |
|------|-------|-----------|----------------|-------------|
| 1 | **SERP check** | ROLE 14 | Target keyword SERP snapshot current; PAA questions captured; featured snippet format identified | Return to ROLE 14 for re-analysis |
| 2 | **AI answer check** | ROLE 15 | Key questions have direct answer blocks; entity references are explicit; citation-ready formatting confirmed | Return to ROLE 3 + ROLE 15 for content update |
| 3 | **Content uniqueness / differentiation check** | ROLE 27 | Page contains original evidence, unique analysis, or non-template sections absent from top 5 competitors | Return to ROLE 3 + ROLE 27 for rewrite |
| 4 | **Evidence check** | ROLE 4 | All key claims backed by `status: "available"` evidence entries; no unsupported bonus amounts; lastChecked dates present | Return to ROLE 4 + ROLE 25 for evidence update |
| 5 | **Offer integrity check** | ROLE 16 | Bonus amounts, promo codes, and reward claims are consistent across page copy, screenshots, and schema `offers.price` | Return to ROLE 16 + ROLE 0 for decision |
| 6 | **Screenshot quality check** | ROLE 18 | All published screenshots are status `"available"`; no error pages, loading states, or wrong locale; URL bars hidden or cropped | Return to ROLE 17 + ROLE 18 for recapture/edit |
| 7 | **Screenshot privacy check** | ROLE 33 | No private data, real UIDs, QR codes, or wallet addresses visible; demo screenshots use demo email only; masking verified | Hard reject by ROLE 33; ROLE 0 must override explicitly |
| 8 | **Visual design check** | ROLE 20 | Desktop layout passes; no screenshot dumps; visual hierarchy is premium; CTA is visible above fold | Return to ROLE 6 + ROLE 20 for layout fix |
| 9 | **Mobile UX check** | ROLE 32 | All key sections readable at 375px; CTAs are tappable; no horizontal scroll; screenshots scale correctly | Return to ROLE 32 + ROLE 7 for mobile fix |
| 10 | **Conversion / CTA check** | ROLE 34 | Primary CTA copy is clear; offer card explains the bonus correctly; no conversion friction before the primary CTA | Return to ROLE 22 + ROLE 34 for CTA revision |
| 11 | **Schema check** | ROLE 8 | Schema validates with no errors; HowTo steps are complete; `offers.price` has `bonusPriceSafe: true` if bonus amount is in schema; FAQ schema present | Return to ROLE 8 for schema fix |
| 12 | **Compliance check** | ROLE 11 | No prohibited wording; risk disclaimers present; no unsafe investment claim language; regional restrictions flagged | Return to ROLE 11 + ROLE 30 for required rewrites |
| 13 | **Performance check** | ROLE 29 | No image over 300KB uncompressed WebP; lazy loading on below-fold images confirmed; no layout shift from late-loading assets | Return to ROLE 29 + ROLE 10 for compression |
| 14 | **Human quality review** | ROLE 21 | Page reads clearly as a real user; screenshots help not distract; CTA feels natural; no obvious confusion points | Return to ROLE 3 + ROLE 35 for revision |
| 15 | **Owner visual approval** | ROLE 0 | Owner has reviewed the live-preview or built page and given explicit written approval | Deploy blocked until ROLE 0 approval in writing |
| 16 | **QA / release approval** | ROLE 9 | Build passes; no console errors; regression checks on 3+ pages; deploy script ready; post-deploy live URLs confirmed | Deploy blocked until ROLE 9 sign-off |

### Check Chain Rules

- **Sequential:** checks run in order 1 → 16. A failed check at step 7 does not allow step 8 to run.
- **No skipping:** all 16 checks are required for Gold Page status. Non-Gold pages may use a reduced gate (ROLE 0 decision).
- **Documented:** the check result for each step must be recorded in the sprint task or a `reports/` QA file.
- **Re-entry:** when a page returns from a failed check, it re-enters the chain at the failed step — not step 1 — unless the fix touches earlier steps.
- **Owner override:** ROLE 0 can explicitly waive a specific check for a named reason. The waiver must be written in the sprint task. Waivers cannot be granted for steps 15 or 16.
